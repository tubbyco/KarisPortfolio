Swiftly is a data-exchange platform that enables seamless, secure sharing of business data between enterprises.

  • Role UI/UX Designer
  • Timeline 6 Months

Project Background

Swiftly was designed to simplify the process of exchanging structured and unstructured business data between enterprises. Our team identified that existing platforms were either too complex or lacked transparency, making it difficult for businesses to collaborate confidently. The goal was to create an intuitive, trustworthy system that reduces friction and improves cross-organisation cooperation.
